Consultation Document · June 2019

Heathrow Airport Expansion

Since our airport operations began over 70 years ago, Heathrow has played a unique role in the development of the surrounding area, and we are proud of the partnerships we share with local communities. Today, we are committed to working closely with our neighbours to develop plans for an expanded airport and ensure that we deliver a fairer Heathrow for the next generation.
