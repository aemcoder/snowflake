    Stardust-Module (faq-accordion)
    titleQuestions? We have answers.
    itemWhat is Adobe LLM Optimizer?Adobe LLM Optimizer is a generative AI search and discovery optimization solution that helps brands improve how their content is cited and surfaced in AI-powered search results. It provides automated insights, recommendations, and optimizations across major LLMs.
    itemHow does Adobe LLM Optimizer benefit from Semrush insights?Adobe LLM Optimizer and Semrush AI Optimization both address AI search visibility, but each uses a distinct data methodology to estimate LLM responses. Adobe LLM Optimizer statistically approximates LLM answers to selected prompts to help predict an AI-model's typical behavior. This model is strengthened by the clickstream data and insights-backed prompt database offered by Semrush.
    itemWhat problem does Adobe LLM Optimizer solve?Websites are seeing decreased click-through rates and engagement as users get answers directly from AI results and generative summaries. LLM Optimizer helps brands ensure they stay visible, cited, and chosen by identifying gaps and providing recommendations to improve discoverability across major LLMs.
    itemWho is Adobe LLM Optimizer for?Adobe LLM Optimizer is for digital marketing, SEO, content, web development, publishing teams, and merchandizing teams who are responsible for creating content, driving website traffic, optimizing search strategies, and managing the discoverability of brands across LLMs.
    itemHow does Adobe LLM Optimizer work?Adobe LLM Optimizer empowers marketers to identify opportunities to drive visibility across LLMs and proactively optimizes content. It leverages the following framework: discover, optimize, and measure.
    itemWhat makes Adobe LLM Optimizer different from traditional SEO tools?Unlike traditional SEO or generative engine optimization (GEO) tools, Adobe LLM Optimizer goes beyond tracking mentions. It diagnoses why content isn't being cited, prescribes what to fix, deploys optimizations directly, and connects AI search visibility to business outcomes.
    itemWhat is an LLM?A large language model (LLM) is an advanced type of AI trained on massive datasets of text to understand and generate human-like language. LLMs power conversational interfaces, AI search, and content generation across enterprise and consumer applications.
    itemWhat are examples of LLMs used in marketing and search?Some widely used LLMs in enterprise marketing and discovery include OpenAI's ChatGPT, Google's Gemini, Anthropic's Claude, Meta's Llama, and Perplexity. Each has distinct capabilities and citation patterns that Adobe LLM Optimizer tracks and optimizes for.
    itemHow are LLMs different from general AI?LLMs are a subset of artificial intelligence, focused specifically on language understanding and generation. While general AI may include vision, robotics, and decision systems, LLMs specialize in interpreting and producing text — which is why they sit at the heart of conversational search and content discovery.
    itemWhy do LLMs matter for digital marketers and content teams?LLMs are redefining how users search and discover content. Instead of clicking through links, users increasingly rely on AI-generated answers — meaning brand visibility, accuracy, and citation across LLMs is now a primary marketing channel.
    itemCan I use Adobe LLM Optimizer with my commerce solution?Yes. Adobe LLM Optimizer is designed to work alongside your existing commerce solution, including Adobe Commerce and other major commerce platforms, to ensure your products and product content are visible across AI-powered shopping channels.
    itemDoes Adobe LLM Optimizer measure traffic from LLMs?Yes. It provides visibility into traffic and engagement originating from AI assistants, agentic browsers, and generative search experiences — connecting those signals to downstream business outcomes when integrated with Adobe Customer Journey Analytics.
