    Stardust-Module (faq-accordion)
    titleFrequently asked questions about Brand Concierge and AI customer experiences.
    itemWhat is Adobe Brand Concierge?Adobe Brand Concierge is a brand-first AI application that transforms your digital properties into dynamic, conversational experiences. Instead of visitors relying on menus or search bars to hunt for information, Brand Concierge guides them through with natural dialogue. Powered by Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator, it anticipates customer needs, answers questions in context, and presents information in a way that feels like speaking directly with the brand.
    itemHow does Adobe Brand Concierge use AI agents?Adobe Brand Concierge is powered by Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator, which coordinates multiple specialized agents to deliver adaptive, brand-aligned experiences that respond intelligently to customer intent. The orchestration layer enables Product Advisor Agent and Site Advisory Agent to work seamlessly together, dynamically routing conversations based on user goals and context.
    itemHow does Brand Concierge improve my customers' experience?Traffic to brand sites is declining as AI assistants drive discovery. Visitors now arrive via AI summaries with clearer intent, making each visit more valuable. Brand Concierge was built for this shift. It greets users, understands intent, and responds using first-party data and approved content. A retail shopper can ask about fall trends and get curated product suggestions, or a software prospect can compare plans with case studies and proof points.
    itemIs Adobe Brand Concierge only for business-to-consumer brands?Adobe Brand Concierge supports both consumer and business buyer journeys. It enhances retail discovery through personalized product recommendations and works equally well for B2B teams qualifying leads, surfacing solution comparisons, and accelerating consideration through context-rich answers.
    itemWhat sets Adobe Brand Concierge apart from AI assistants and large language models?AI assistants are often the first stop for customers, but their answers are generic and disconnected from your brand. They can't represent your products with the nuance, context, or accuracy that customers expect. Brand Concierge is grounded in your first-party data and approved content — every answer is on-brand, accurate, and actionable.
    itemIs Adobe Brand Concierge safe for my brand?Yes. Brand Concierge is built with trust at its core. Every interaction is grounded in first-party data and approved content. Brand attributes function as guardrails so that tone, claims, and recommendations stay consistent — and the platform meets enterprise standards for compliance and content safety.
    itemCan marketing teams manage Brand Concierge without developers?Yes. Brand Concierge is built to be managed directly by marketing teams. A low-code environment allows marketers to update brand attributes, refine voice, configure response patterns, and adjust guardrails without engineering support.
    itemHow does Brand Concierge build long-term customer relationships?Brand Concierge uses your company's first-party data and approved content to deliver personalized, context-rich interactions across every touchpoint. Each conversation deepens the customer profile in Adobe Experience Platform, so subsequent visits feel familiar — the experience evolves with the relationship.
    itemDo I need to be an existing Adobe customer to use Brand Concierge?No. You do not need to be an existing Adobe customer to get started with Brand Concierge. It can be adopted on its own, and integrates more deeply with Adobe Experience Platform, Adobe Experience Manager, Adobe Commerce, and other Adobe products as your needs grow.
