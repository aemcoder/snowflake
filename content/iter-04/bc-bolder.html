Stardust-Module (faq-accordion)
titleFrequently asked questions.
itemWhat is Adobe Brand Concierge?Adobe Brand Concierge is a brand-first AI application that transforms your digital properties into dynamic, conversational experiences. Instead of visitors relying on menus or search bars to hunt for information, Brand Concierge guides them through with natural dialogue.
itemHow does Adobe Brand Concierge use AI agents?Adobe Brand Concierge is powered by Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator, which coordinates multiple specialized agents to deliver adaptive, brand-aligned experiences that respond intelligently to customer intent.
itemHow does Brand Concierge improve my customers' experience?Traffic to brand sites is declining as AI assistants drive discovery. Visitors now arrive via AI summaries with clearer intent. Brand Concierge greets users, understands intent, and responds using first-party data and approved content.
itemIs Adobe Brand Concierge safe for my brand?Yes. Brand Concierge is built with trust at its core. Every interaction is grounded in first-party data and approved content. Brand attributes function as guardrails so that tone, claims, and recommendations stay consistent.
itemCan marketing teams manage Brand Concierge without developers?Yes. Brand Concierge is built to be managed directly by marketing teams. A low-code environment allows marketers to update brand attributes, refine voice, configure response patterns, and adjust guardrails without engineering support.
itemDo I need to be an existing Adobe customer to use Brand Concierge?No. You do not need to be an existing Adobe customer to get started with Brand Concierge. It can be adopted on its own, and integrates more deeply with Adobe Experience Platform, Adobe Experience Manager, Adobe Commerce, and other Adobe products as your needs grow.
